Hello, I'm Kansuke Ito. Welcome to my cultural connections page!

The top two cultural shocks I experienced when moving to Vancouver, Canada.

  1. Cannabis is legal.

    2. I see people smoking marijuana and freezing up in various places downtown. It's a sight you wouldn't see in Japan.

  2. Frequent bus delays

    3. In Japan, buses arrive on time, but it is rare for buses to arrive on time in Vancouver. Besides, passengers behave as if nothing had happened. I was surprised.

The top two things I miss most from Japan.

  1. Japanese tea

    2. I love Japanese tea. When I was in Japan, I drank many kinds of Japanese tea, but the number of Japanese tea sold downtown is limited. And the price is high.

  2. sushi

    3. I also love sushi. I really miss sushi with fresh raw fish and Japanese rice. I especially want to eat Negitoro sushi.

The most amazing new things I love about Vancouver or Canada in general.

  1. Nature at its best

    2. Canada's spectacular wilderness has amazed me many times. I especially like LynCannyon, I think the depth and mystery of that forest atmosphere is so wonderful!

  2. Interethnic nation

    3. Canada is made up of many different ethnic groups. And people from many different countries come to Canada. I am very happy to be able to experience the culture of many different countries.
